Who We Are

We are a full-service law firm based in Surrey and Abbotsford, British Columbia.

We have over 20 years of experience in Criminal Law, Immigration Law, Family Law, and Mediation.

We have a broad based service capability to handhold our clients at various stages of their personal requirements with a team of professionals handpicked from the best institutions and organizations across the globe, the Firm provides cutting edge advice by blending its legal proficiency with deep legal insight. The Firm boasts of experts who have substantial experience in their respective practice areas. The Firm provides focus driven services to clients where lawyers integrate their skills to provide comprehensive legal advice and strategy on complex legal issues that meets the clients’ needs and expectations.

Why Work With Us

Sandhu Law and Mediation offers a comprehensive range of legal services to its clients. Whether you are a newcomer navigating the immigration process or someone facing criminal charges and need a strong legal defense, or seeking to resolve a delicate family matter, our team of professionals and their client-centered approach will deliver creative yet practical solutions to your legal challenges.
